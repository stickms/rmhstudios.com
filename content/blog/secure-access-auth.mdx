# Secure Access: Authentication Update

At RMH Studios, we take your gaming experience and data security seriously. That's why we're excited to announce the rollout of our new authentication system across all our games.

## Why the Change?

Previously, games like *Echoes of the Spire* and *Laundry Sort* allowed for guest play with manual username entry. While convenient, this meant that:
- Your scores weren't permanently linked to you.
- Someone else could claim your username.
- Progress couldn't be synced across devices.

## What's New?

### 1. Unified Login
We've integrated a robust authentication system using **Better Auth**. You can now sign in using your Discord account or email/password. This single identity works across our entire platform.

### 2. Progress Synchronization
No more losing your high scores when you switch browsers or clear your cache. Your achievements in *Slice It!* and *Laundry Sort* are now tied to your secure account.

### 3. Fair Play
By verifying identities, we're taking a big step towards ensuring fair play on our leaderboards. We can now better prevent score spoofing and ensure that the top spots are held by legitimate players.

## Getting Started

If you haven't already, simply click the "Sign In" button on any game start screen. It takes just a few seconds to set up, and you'll be ready to secure your legacy on the leaderboards.
