# Redefining the Vision

For months, *Echoes* was a 3D tactical shooter. We had the movement, the recoil, and the atmospheric lighting of a AAA title. But something was missing: the "game."

Processing narrative depth in a first-person perspective often leads to a disconnect between high-octane action and subtle storytelling. By transitioning to a top-down 2D perspective, we've found a way to marry "Vampire Survivors" style intensity with the complex RPG elements our community loves.

## What's Changed?
- **Tactical Clarity**: You can now see the entire battlefield, allowing for better class synergy.
* **Narrative Overlays**: We've introduced Visual Novel-style dialogue prompts that feel natural without breaking the flow.
* **Performance**: The game now runs smoothly on almost any device, including mobile.

The 2D Alpha is just the beginning of this new chapter.
