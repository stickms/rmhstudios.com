We're excited to unveil our latest prototype: **Slice It!**

It's a high-energy rhythm game that runs directly in your browser. No downloads, no installation—just pure, uncut beats.

## The Concept

We wanted to build something that feels responsive and "juicy" without relying on heavy game engines like Unity or Unreal. Using the **Web Audio API** for precise timing and HTML5 Canvas for rendering, we've achieved a smooth 60fps experience on most devices.

### Core Features at Launch:
- **Precise Timing**: Audio scheduling is handled on a separate thread logic to ensure notes hit exactly when they should.
- **Dynamic Visuals**: Neon aesthetics that react to the music.
- **Combo System**: Rack up points and keep the multiplier going!

Give it a try and let us know what you think. We have big plans for future updates, including custom track uploads and more complex note types.
