# Verified Leaderboards Are Here

The competition is heating up at RMH Studios. With our latest update, we've overhauled how leaderboards work to ensure integrity and reliability.

## No More "Guest" Champions

We've all seen it—leaderboards filled with "Guest123" or duplicated names. That ends today. Our new API strictly requires a valid user session to accept any score submission.

This means:
- **Unique Identities**: Each name on the leaderboard corresponds to a verified unique user.
- **Persistent Stats**: Your total kills in *Echoes*, your laundry folding efficiency, and your slicing precision are tracked over time.
- **Global Recognition**: When you reach the top, everyone knows it's really you.

## Transitioning Your Scores

If you've been playing as a guest with a specific username, don't worry! If you register an account with that same username (and no one else has claimed it), we'll link your past guest scores to your new verified account.

It's time to prove your skills. See you on the leaderboards!
