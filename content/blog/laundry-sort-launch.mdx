We're thrilled to announce **Laundry Sort**, our newest browser-based game! 

It's a physics-driven experience that combines the relaxing satisfaction of organizing with the chaotic fun of watching fabric tumble through the air. No installation needed—just open it in your browser and start sorting.

## The Concept

Have you ever just wanted to watch clothes fall from the sky and bounce into the right bins? Well, now you can. Laundry Sort is built on the premise that sorting doesn't have to be boring—it can be vibrant, dynamic, and weirdly satisfying.

### Core Features at Launch:
- **Physics-Based Gameplay**: Every piece of laundry has physics! They tumble, bounce, and roll realistically using the Rapier physics engine.
- **Color-Matched Bins**: Watch as your clothes tumble toward their matching color bins. Red goes to red, blue to blue, and so on.
- **Score & Penalty System**: Earn 100 points for correct sorts, but lose 25 for mismatches. It's all about accuracy and speed.
- **2-Minute Rush**: Can you sort the most laundry in 120 seconds? Compete on the leaderboard!
- **Beautiful Neon Aesthetics**: A vibrant, modern UI that makes organizing feel like playing.

## The Challenge

The real trick is that gravity doesn't discriminate. Your clothes tumble down randomly, and you can't control their trajectory—only watch and hope they land in the right bin. It's a game of observation, pattern recognition, and a little bit of luck.

## What's Next?

We're planning to expand Laundry Sort with new features:
- **Multiple Game Modes**: Timed challenges, endless mode, and relaxation mode
- **Customizable Clothing**: More diverse laundry items and patterns
- **Power-ups**: Special items that change how physics work
- **Multiplayer Leaderboards**: Compete globally and see who's the best sorter

Give it a try and let us know what you think. Sorting has never been this fun!
