One of the biggest challenges in rhythm games is content. Hand-crafting levels takes hours.

So we taught the computer to do it for us.

## The Algorithm

Our new **BeatMapGenerator** analyzes the raw audio buffer of any uploaded track to detect peaks and rhythmic onsets.

### Stereo Awareness
But we didn't just want random notes. The generator analyzes the **Left** and **Right** audio channels independently.
- Loud signal on Left? Note goes to **Top Lane**.
- Loud signal on Right? Note goes to **Bottom Lane**.
- Balanced signal? It alternates or creates a double hit.

This means if you upload a track with distinct stereo separation (like old Beatles tracks or modern EDM), the gameplay actually reflects the spatial audio.
