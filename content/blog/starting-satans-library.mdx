Today marks the official beginning of development for our most ambitious project yet: **Satan's Library**. 

We've spent the last 24 hours prototyping the core movement mechanics, but the real star of the show is our new camera system.

## The Camera System

We aimed for a classic survival horror feel but modernized for 2026. The camera isn't just a static observer; it reacts to the player's stress levels (Aura stats).

### Key Features:
- **Dynamic Angles**: smoothly transitions between fixed angles and over-the-shoulder depending on the room tightness.
- **Stress Shake**: As your "Lock In" meter depletes, the camera handheld shake increases.
- **Occlusion Handling**: No more getting stuck behind walls.

We're incredibly excited to share more as we build out the library environment. Stay locked in.
