# The Shadow in the Machine

The Phantom wasn't just built for speed; it was built for the *feeling* of being untouchable. 

## Identity via Mechanics
The core of the Phantom is the **Blink** ability. Initially a simple teleport, we refined it into a high-speed dash with a ghosting effect. Why? Because the travel time—even just 150 milliseconds—gives the player a sense of physical presence.

## Abilities
- **Shadow Burst**: A localized AoE that clears breathing room.
- **Soul Drain**: A passive that rewards aggressive play with health-on-kill.

Balancing the "glass cannon" nature of the Phantom requires precise feedback loops. If you die, it should feel like *your* mistake, not the game's.
