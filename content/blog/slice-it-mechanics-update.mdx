The feedback on **Slice It** has been amazing, but you asked for more challenge. We heard you.

Today's update introduces two major mechanics that fundamentally change how the game is played.

## The Health System

We've moved away from a simple "fail on miss" approach to a more forgiving (but stressful) health bar system, similar to **Osu!**.
- **Miss a note**: Lose 15% health.
- **Hit a note**: Recover a small amount.
- **Reach 0%**: System Failure.

This allows players to recover from a bad slip-up if they can regain their rhythm quickly.

## Stereo Lanes

Why stick to one lane when you have two ears? We've split the track into **Top** and **Bottom** lanes.
- **'F' Key**: Controls the Top Lane.
- **'J' Key**: Controls the Bottom Lane.

This adds a layer of coordination complexity that feels incredibly satisfying once you lock in.
