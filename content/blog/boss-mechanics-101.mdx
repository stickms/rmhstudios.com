# The Titan Approaches

In *Echoes*, time is your greatest enemy. Every 2 minutes, a Boss entity spawns to test your build's viability.

## Scaling Difficulty
We don't just increase health. Bosses in the late game possess:
- **Bullet Novas**: 360-degree projectile bursts that force movement.
* **Aggressive Dashing**: High-speed maneuvers that can cut off escape routes.
* **Quadratic Stat Growth**: HP scales faster than player damage, making late-game encounters a genuine struggle for survival.

## Phase State Machine
Bosses cycle through three primary states:
1. **Chasing**: standard movement toward the player.
2. **Charging**: preparing for a high-damage dash.
3. **Bursting**: unleashing a hail of projectiles.

Survive the boss, and the rewards (massive XP drops) will push you toward the next level of evolution.
