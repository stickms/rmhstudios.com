Day 2 of development on **Satan's Library**. After nailing the camera system yesterday, today was all about transitions.

## The Door Problem

In survival horror, doors are more than just portals; they are loading screens, pacing mechanisms, and sources of tension. We wanted to keep that tension without the loading times.

### Implementation Details

We're using a seamless streaming tech that pre-loads the adjacent room as you approach the door handle.

- **Interaction**: You have to physically rotate the analog stick (or mouse) to turn the handle, adding to the immersion.
- **Creak Physics**: The speed of the door opening affects the sound it makes using our custom audio engine.
