SUBORDINATE DIRECTORATE · CODENAME: THE QUIET DEPLOY

RMHStapo — Secret Police

The RMHStapo is the enforcement arm of the Department. It exists to identify, detain, and correct any personnel found responsible for slow loading, failed deploys, unoptimized assets, or render-blocking stylesheets. It maintains the roster of those who have pushed 380 KB of blocking CSS to production, and it visits them. It administers corrective exile in coordination with the Department of the Navy. It knocks softly, and only once.

As of 19 July 2026, the RMHStapo is also your Human Resources.

Whereas morale, like latency, is a metric; and whereas the record of events has on occasion differed from the events themselves, to the detriment of morale — it is hereby ordered that there be established within the Department of Internal Affairs a Department of Propaganda.

The Department shall maintain the record of the Department's successes and shall correct the record of everything else. It shall produce slogans, banners, and commemorative material for occasions of restoration. Its first finding is attached, and is final: the website has always been fast.
