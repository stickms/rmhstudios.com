# 🍔 Food Delivery Web App — Feature Ideas

---

## 🧱 Core / Essential Features

- ✅ **User Registration & Authentication** — Guest mode (no auth required); mock user profile with saved addresses and payment methods stored locally
- ✅ **Restaurant Listings Page** — Browse all available restaurants with name, cuisine type, rating, and estimated delivery time
- ✅ **Restaurant Detail Page** — View full menu organized by category (appetizers, mains, drinks, desserts, etc.)
- ✅ **Menu Item Detail Modal** — Expand an item to see description, price, calories, allergens, and customization options
- ✅ **Shopping Cart** — Add/remove items, adjust quantities, and review your order before checkout
- ✅ **Checkout Flow** — Enter delivery address, choose payment method, review order summary, and place order
- ✅ **Order Confirmation Page** — Summary of placed order with estimated delivery time and order ID
- ✅ **Order History** — View past orders with item breakdowns, total cost, and order date
- ✅ **Basic Search** — Search restaurants or menu items by name
- ✅ **Delivery Address Management** — Save and manage multiple delivery addresses
- ✅ **Payment Method Management** — Save credit/debit cards or link PayPal/Stripe
- ✅ **Order Status Tracker** — Real-time status updates (Order Received → Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered)
- ✅ **Ratings & Reviews** — Rate and review restaurants and individual dishes after delivery
- ✅ **Restaurant Categories / Cuisine Filters** — Filter by cuisine type (Chinese, Italian, Vegan, etc.)
- ✅ **Basic Notifications** — In-app toast alerts for order confirmation, dispatch, and delivery status changes

---

## ✨ Quality of Life (QOL) Features

- **Reorder Button** — One-click reorder of a previous order
- **Favorites / Saved Restaurants** — Bookmark restaurants for quick access
- **Saved Orders / "Usual Order"** — Save a specific cart configuration to reorder repeatedly
- **Estimated Delivery Time Display** — Show estimated delivery time on restaurant cards before selecting
- **Live Order Tracking Map** — See your delivery driver's real-time location on a map
- **Driver Info Card** — See driver name, photo, and vehicle info once dispatched
- **Contact Driver Button** — Call or message the driver directly through the app
- **Tip Customization** — Set a custom tip percentage or flat amount at checkout
- **Scheduled Orders** — Place an order now for a later delivery time
- **Group Order Mode** — Invite friends/coworkers to add items to a shared cart before checkout
- **Promo Code / Coupon Input** — Apply discount codes at checkout
- **Loyalty Points System** — Earn points per order, redeem for discounts
- **Referral Program** — Share a referral link and earn credits when someone places their first order
- **Dietary Preference Tags** — Tag items as Vegan, Gluten-Free, Halal, Kosher, Nut-Free, etc.
- **Allergen Warnings** — Display allergen information prominently on menu items
- **Dark Mode** — Toggle between light and dark UI themes
- **Multi-language Support** — Switch the UI between supported languages
- **Accessibility Mode** — High contrast, larger fonts, and screen reader support
- **Order Issue Reporting** — Report missing items, wrong orders, or damaged food with a few taps
- **Refund Request Flow** — Seamlessly request a refund for an issue with a clear status tracker
- **SMS Notifications** — Opt-in to receive order updates via text message
- **Push Notifications (PWA)** — Browser push notifications for order updates
- **"Leave at Door" Option** — Contactless delivery instructions
- **Special Delivery Instructions Field** — Add custom notes for the driver or restaurant
- **Minimum Order Display** — Clearly show restaurant minimum order requirements before browsing
- **Delivery Fee Transparency** — Break down fees (delivery, service, small order) clearly before checkout
- **Estimated Calories on Cart** — Show a running calorie total as you build your order
- **Recently Viewed Restaurants** — Quick access panel for restaurants you've browsed recently

## 🛠️ Cool Tools & Advanced Features

- **AI Meal Recommender** — Get personalized restaurant and dish suggestions based on past orders and preferences
- **"Surprise Me" Button** — Randomly pick a highly-rated restaurant and auto-populate a recommended order
- **Smart Reorder Suggestions** — App suggests your likely order at your go-to spot based on time of day and day of week
- **Mood-Based Ordering** — Pick a mood (Comfort Food, Healthy, Adventurous, Hangover Fix) and get curated suggestions
- **Calorie Budget Planner** — Set a daily calorie target and browse only meals that fit within your remaining budget
- **Split Bill Tool** — Automatically split a group order's total across multiple people with their respective items
- **Real-Time Restaurant Busyness Indicator** — Show how busy a kitchen is right now and adjust ETAs dynamically
- **Live Menu Availability** — Mark items as sold out in real-time so users can't order unavailable dishes
- **Order Timeline Playback** — After delivery, see a full timeline of your order's lifecycle with timestamps
- **Dietary Profile Builder** — Build a full dietary profile (allergies, restrictions, goals) and have menus auto-filtered
- **Ingredient-Level Search** — Search for dishes by a specific ingredient ("show me everything with avocado")
- **Price Comparison Mode** — See the same dish across multiple restaurants and compare prices side by side
- **"What's Fastest Near Me" View** — Sort all restaurants by current estimated delivery time, not default ranking
- **Subscription / Meal Plan** — Subscribe to weekly meal deliveries from a curated restaurant rotation
- **Virtual Food Court** — Order from multiple restaurants in a single checkout with combined delivery
- **Restaurant Owner Dashboard** — Separate portal for restaurant owners to manage menus, hours, and incoming orders
- **Driver Analytics Dashboard** — Driver-facing app with earnings, route history, and performance stats
- **Heatmap Order Insights (Admin)** — Admin view showing popular ordering zones, peak hours, and item trends
- **Dynamic Surge Pricing Display** — Clearly communicate when delivery fees are elevated and estimated return to normal
- **Photo Menu Mode** — Browse an entirely visual, photo-first menu layout (no text cards)
- **AR Dish Preview** — View an augmented reality 3D model of a dish before ordering (mobile)
- **Chatbot Support** — AI-powered chat support for tracking, refunds, and FAQs without contacting a human
- **Carbon Footprint Indicator** — Show an estimated carbon footprint score per order with eco-friendly badges
- **Eco-Friendly Packaging Filter** — Filter restaurants by those using sustainable or compostable packaging
- **Donation Rounding** — Round up your order total to the nearest dollar and donate the change to a charity
- **Order for Others (Gift Delivery)** — Send a meal to a friend with a custom message, paid by you
- **Interactive Nutrition Panel** — Tap any menu item to see a full nutrition label breakdown (macros, sodium, etc.)
- **Restaurant Loyalty Cards** — Restaurant-specific stamp cards that reward frequent customers with free items
- **Live Chat with Restaurant** — Message the restaurant directly to ask questions before ordering
- **Waitlist / Pre-Order for Closed Restaurants** — Join a queue to order from a restaurant not yet open
- **Trending Dishes Feed** — A social-style feed showing what's popular in your area right now
- **User Food Journal** — Automatically log orders into a personal food journal with nutritional summaries over time
