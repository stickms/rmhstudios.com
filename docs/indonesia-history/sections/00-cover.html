Indonesia
A Comprehensive History

From the deep prehistory of the archipelago — Java Man, the Austronesian voyagers, the Hindu-Buddhist kingdoms of Srivijaya and Majapahit — through the Islamic sultanates, the Dutch centuries, the national revolution, and the making of the modern Republic, to the Indonesia of the present day. With maps, figures, cited sources, and end-of-chapter review questions.

About this book. This is a teaching text and a reference: a single, continuous narrative of the Indonesian archipelago and its peoples across more than a million years, written for the serious general reader. It synthesizes the work of professional historians and primary scholarship, cites its sources, and flags where the historical record is thin or contested.
