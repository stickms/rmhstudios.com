## GLOSSARY

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Densus 88 | (*Detasemen Khusus 88* — Special Detachment 88) Indonesia's elite counter-terrorism police unit, established June 2003 after the 2002 Bali bombings; named for the 88 Australian victims. |
| DPR | (*Dewan Perwakilan Rakyat* — People's Representative Council) The lower house of Indonesia's parliament; under the reformed constitution, directly elected. |
| GAM | (*Gerakan Aceh Merdeka* — Free Aceh Movement) Acehnese independence organization founded 1976; signed Helsinki MOU 2005 accepting self-government within Indonesia. |
| Jemaah Islamiyah | ("Islamic community") Islamist militant network formally established in 1993 by Abu Bakar Ba'asyir and Abdullah Sungkar; responsible for the 2002 Bali bombings and subsequent attacks; announced dissolution in 2024. |
| KPK | (*Komisi Pemberantasan Korupsi* — Corruption Eradication Commission) Indonesian anti-corruption agency created by Law No. 30/2002 under Megawati; operational from 2003; equipped with independent investigative and prosecutorial powers. |
| Nahdlatul Ulama | ("Revival of the Religious Scholars") Indonesia's largest Islamic organization, founded 1926; traditionalist, pesantren-based; claimed ~40 million members in the Wahid era; its political wing PKB emerged after 1998. |
| Tipikor | (*Pengadilan Tindak Pidana Korupsi* — Corruption Crimes Court) Specialized anti-corruption tribunal established under SBY to try KPK-prosecuted cases. |
