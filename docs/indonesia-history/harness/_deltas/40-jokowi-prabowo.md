## GLOSSARY

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| blusukan | An Indonesian term for an impromptu, unannounced inspection visit by a public official to an ordinary neighbourhood or workplace; popularised as a governing style by Joko Widodo as Solo mayor and Jakarta governor. |
| KPK | *Komisi Pemberantasan Korupsi* — the Corruption Eradication Commission, an independent anti-corruption body established in 2002 as part of post-Suharto institutional reform; controversially restructured and subordinated to the executive branch by legislation passed in 2019. |
| Nusantara (new capital) | Indonesia's planned new national capital in the Penajam North Paser and Kutai Kartanegara regencies of East Kalimantan, formally named and legalised in 2022 at an estimated cost of Rp 523 trillion (~US$35 billion). Distinct from the classical archipelago concept. |
| Gerindra | *Partai Gerakan Indonesia Raya* (Greater Indonesia Movement Party), the nationalist political party founded by Prabowo Subianto in 2008, through which he contested the 2014, 2019, and 2024 presidential elections. |
| PDI-P | *Partai Demokrasi Indonesia – Perjuangan* (Indonesian Democratic Party of Struggle), the party of Megawati Sukarnoputri that backed Jokowi's 2014 and 2019 presidential bids. |
| Danantara | *Daya Anagata Nusantara*, a sovereign wealth fund established by the Prabowo government in February 2025 to consolidate and manage assets of Indonesian state-owned enterprises, with an initial estimated US$900 billion under management. |
